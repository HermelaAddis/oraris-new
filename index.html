About Us

Oraris Trade and Industry PLC

Trading PLC

Our company Oraris Trade and Industry PLC established in 2014 and working on Trade, Manufacturing and Consultancy business. Our consultancy business mainly focuses on consulting Ethiopian students on how to join international colleges and universities. We are pleased to inform you that it is organizing international universities expo, which is scheduled to take place from July 26-30, 2024 at Addis Ababa Millennium Hall. The international University Expo aims to provide a platform for students, parents, and educational professionals to explore various opportunities for higher education. It would bring together renowned universities, colleges, and education institutions offering a diverse range of academic program. The expo will serve as an informative and interactive platform where attendees can directly engage with representatives from there institutions, obtain valuable guidance, and gain insights in to the education system. The expo is expected to attract over 20,000 attendees from parents, students and education experts.
